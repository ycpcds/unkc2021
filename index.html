Welcome to our website. We have created this artefact for the summative assessment section of the Understanding The Nature and Knowledge of Curriculum course, as part of the MSc Transformative Learning and Teaching degree programme.

Our Aim

Our curriculum for 2035 will facilitate the development of learners with the critical capacities necessary to intervene in systemic power, build alternative power, and achieve liberation from oppressive systems. We believe that this requires us to teach for both access to hegemonic knowledge and for the production of alternative knowledge. We propose that the ideal curriculum would facilitate the development of Critical Digital Literacy (CDL).
